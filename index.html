Biography

Hello, my name is 陈爽. I am a fifth-year Ph.D. candidate in the Computer Systems Lab at Cornell University, advised by Prof. José F. Martínez and working closely with Prof. Christina Delimitrou. My research focuses on scheduling and managing interactive services in datacenters. Currently, I am working on improving performance of interactive services by leveraging emerging memory technologies.
